About the Owner

Romeo Adams

Born 3 October 1970 • Bellville, Cape Town

Romeo Adams Consulting Services is an owner-managed consultancy, providing predominantly human resource management and development consulting, but also providing project management and parliamentary practice and procedure consulting.

Romeo Adams is a professionally registered, qualified and experienced Human Resources Executive aiming to develop and make a difference in the lives of previously disadvantaged communities. The consultancy establishes relationships with previously disadvantaged communities and stakeholders who want to contribute towards the development of such communities.

Romeo is a seasoned executive leader with over two decades of strategic management experience across South Africa’s public sector. His career spans high-impact roles equivalent to Director-General and Chief Executive Officer, including his most recent tenure as Secretary to the Western Cape Provincial Parliament (2019–2025).

Known for his exceptional analytical acumen, emotional intelligence, and visionary leadership, Romeo consistently aligns institutional goals with transformative action. He holds a Master’s Degree in Industrial Psychology and has led diverse portfolios in justice, correctional services, tourism, and legislative governance.

Romeo’s core strengths include strategic planning, financial oversight, human capital management and development, and stakeholder engagement at the highest levels—ranging from parliamentary authorities to international organisations.

He is a published thought leader and international speaker, contributing to global dialogues on legislative modernisation, cybersecurity, and executive accountability. His governance expertise is further reflected in board memberships across education, sport, and public service institutions. A registered Master HR Professional with the South African Board of People Practices, Romeo blends technical mastery with a deep commitment to service delivery innovation and institutional excellence.

Outside of his professional realm, Romeo is a passionate sports administrator and coach, having led rugby and baseball federations with the same rigor and pride he brings to public service. His leadership is defined by integrity, resilience, and a relentless pursuit of progress.

About the owner

Romeo Adams, born on 3 October 1970 in Bellville, Cape Town, is the youngest of five children. He grew up in Elsies River, matriculated in 1989 at Valhalla Senior Secondary School, and is married to Hayley, with his three sons: Rory, Reece, and Romano.

Deeply rooted in his community, Romeo is committed to contributing meaningfully to the upliftment of previously disadvantaged communities and remains actively engaged in initiatives that foster sustainable development in Elsies River.

He began his career as a male nurse at Tygerberg Hospital before pursuing studies in Public Administration, which set him on a path of leadership in the public service. With a strong focus on people development, he has built an extensive career as a professionally registered Human Resources Executive, serving communities in the Western Cape, Limpopo, and Gauteng since 1989.

Rising through the administrative ranks, Romeo has consistently championed transformation and capacity-building. His expertise and dedication were further recognised in 2015, when he was appointed as an Executive Committee Member of the Public Service Coordinating Bargaining Council (PSCBC), the central bargaining forum for the South African Public Service.
